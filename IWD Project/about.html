Senior Pastor Daniel Musonda Kaira

Founder & Lead Pastor of The Household of Faith Church

Pastor Daniel Musonda Kaira is the visionary founder and Lead Pastor of The Household Of Faith Church (HoF), a fast-growing global ministry with over 2,500 members and more than 6 branches in Zambia, along with a vibrant virtual church for members in the diaspora.

He is renowned for his dynamic preaching, teaching, and gospel songwriting. His mission is to help people discover and confidently live out their God-given purpose through the transformative power of the Gospel. He is best known for teaching on Character Development, Personal Development, and Relationships.

Pastor Daniel’s ministry began during his time as an undergraduate at the University of Zambia in 2016, where he led a mentorship class and co-founded a movement called Word Impowerment Movement (WEM) alongside Apostle Fredrick Kaluluma of City of The Lord Church.

This mentorship class evolved into what is now HoF — a global church focused on teaching, missions, and empowering believers to cause impact in every area of influence.
